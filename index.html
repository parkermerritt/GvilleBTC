What is Bitcoin?

The Internet of Money

Bitcoin is an innovative peer-to-peer payment protocol and a new kind of money. Exchanges can be carried out without third parties; no single central bank or corporation operates the network. Instead, users collaborate worldwide in order to confirm transactions, allowing for exciting new applications and uses of digital money.

Learn more at    www.bitcoin.org.
